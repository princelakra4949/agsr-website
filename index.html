Our Story

Empowering Champions Since 2016

Aryan Gateways Shooting Range is Sonipat's most trusted shooting academy, delivering world-class training for over 9 years. Affiliated with the National Rifle Association of India (NRAI), Haryana Rifle Association, and following ISSF standards, we are committed to producing state and national-level champions.

Our modern facility features electronic scoring systems, professional lanes, and experienced coaches who have guided shooters to podiums at district, state, and national competitions.

Training Programs

Our Olympic Disciplines

All three events are ISSF-standard Olympic shooting disciplines, practised at AGSR Sonipat with professional equipment and certified coaching.

10m
10m Air Pistol / Rifle
Indoor · Electronic Targets

The most popular Olympic shooting discipline. Shoot at 10-metre electronic targets with precision air pistols or rifles. Perfect for all ages including beginners. Our 2 electronic target machines give instant scoring feedback.

Beginner Friendly Olympic Event Electronic Scoring
25m
25m Rapid Fire Pistol
Semi-Outdoor · Standard Targets

A thrilling Olympic event demanding speed, precision and mental composure. Train in 25-metre pistol shooting — an intermediate discipline that bridges the gap between 10m and 50m events.

Intermediate Level Olympic Event
50m
50m Free Pistol
Outdoor · Precision Shooting

The pinnacle of Olympic pistol shooting — a test of ultimate precision and mental strength at 50 metres. Reserved for advanced competitive shooters aiming for state and national championships.

Advanced Level Olympic Event
