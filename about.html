Aryan Gateways Shooting Range

Aryan Gateways Shooting Range (AGSR) Sonipat is one of Haryana's premier and most trusted shooting academies. Founded in 2016 with a vision to make competitive shooting accessible to everyone, we have spent over 9 years producing district, state, and national-level champions from the Sonipat region.

We are officially affiliated with the National Rifle Association of India (NRAI) and the Haryana Rifle Association (HRA), and all our training follows strict ISSF (International Shooting Sport Federation) standards — the same rules used at the Olympic Games.

Our modern indoor range features 2 electronic target machines that deliver real-time decimal scoring, matching competition conditions exactly. We train students in three Olympic disciplines: 10m Air Pistol/Rifle, 25m Pistol, and 50m Free Pistol.

🚫 Busting the Myth About Cost

Many Indian parents believe shooting sport is prohibitively expensive. This is completely false. At AGSR Sonipat, beginner shooters don't need to buy any pistol. We provide 5 academy air pistols absolutely free for training. Your child can start competing without any equipment investment. Shooting is as affordable as cricket or badminton.

🔫

5 Pistols Available Free for Beginners

Walk in on Day 1 with zero equipment. Our 5 academy air pistols are available at no charge for all beginners and intermediate students. You only need to consider your own pistol once you reach the competitive level — and even then, we guide you on the most affordable options.
